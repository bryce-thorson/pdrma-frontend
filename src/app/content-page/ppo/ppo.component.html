PPO

PDRMA Health offers agencies the ability to select from several different comprehensive PPO options, including plans that pair with a Health Reimbursement Account (HRA). Your Benefits Coordinator can provide you with more information about which deductible plan and/or HRA pairing your agency offers.

You can find answers to many questions about the PPO plan in your PDRMA Health Program Plan Document, or you can ask your Benefits Coordinator for a hard copy.
